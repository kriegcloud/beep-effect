# D02 — Executive Technical Synthesis

## Problem Framing

The corpus addresses a recurring delivery failure mode: technical narratives lose credibility when architecture, methods, contracts, and governance claims are not traceable to source artifacts. The canonical approach in this spec package is to treat the corpus as a phase-gated, evidence-linked system rather than a free-form narrative bundle.

## Primary Topic

T02 — executive synthesis and thesis narrative.

## Audience

- Leadership
- White-paper authors
- Technical leads

## Scope Boundaries

### Must include

1. Problem framing and approach thesis.
2. Architecture differentiators with cross-links.
3. Major claims with downstream document pointers.

### Must exclude

1. Exhaustive interface listings (delegated to D08).
2. Full risk governance details (delegated to D11).

## Approach Summary

The corpus synthesis strategy is deterministic-first and phase-structured:

1. Establish deterministic source and evidence surfaces.
2. Normalize terminology and taxonomy before narrative drafting.
3. Separate architecture, methods, reasoning, and contracts into primary ownership documents.
4. Keep all normative claims linked to evidence IDs and status-labeled.

## Architecture Narrative

### System posture

The architecture is not presented as a single static diagram. It is represented as a gated progression (`P0`-`P7`) where each phase introduces bounded artifacts and explicit pass/fail criteria.

### Differentiator 1: Certainty-tiered claims

The corpus preserves an explicit certainty tier model. Deterministic facts and type-system inferences are separated from LLM-inferred claims, enabling leadership-grade risk framing without conflating confidence classes.

### Differentiator 2: Status-labeled synthesis surfaces

Claims are classified as `implemented`, `specified`, or `conceptual`, which prevents narrative overstatement and supports staged roadmap communication.

### Differentiator 3: Operationally bounded AI controls

The operational control strategy defines token budgeting, timeout behavior, and concurrency limits, so AI-assisted extraction behavior is specified as a bounded system rather than unbounded generation.

### Differentiator 4: Governance and quality linkage

Audit findings and gate criteria are explicitly integrated into the corpus model, allowing decision-makers to see unresolved risk before publication handoff.
