# P1 Section Contracts

## Contract Rules

1. Every section includes explicit `Claim IDs` and `Evidence IDs` lines.
2. Every section includes status-label and certainty-tier aware language where maturity is discussed.
3. Every normative statement is grounded in at least one listed claim/evidence pair.
4. Unsupported statements are moved to assumptions and excluded from normative claims.
5. Caveat carry (`C-002` / `E-S03-005`, D11 governance risks) is mandatory in S09 and S10.
