# 02 - Mid / Large / Enterprise Time Platforms

Researched: 2026-06-18

## What "Top" Means Here

For mid-sized and enterprise firms, "top" means finance/practice-management
presence, lawyer adoption, passive capture, compliance validation, prebill and
e-billing workflows, Microsoft 365/DMS integration, and integration with
systems of record.

For agents/developers, "top" means public integration surfaces or at least a
clear partner ecosystem that Beep can treat as an export/integration boundary.

## Short Ranking

Best enterprise comparators:

1. **Intapp Time** - strongest public comparator for AI-assisted time capture,
   compliance, and cloud legal timekeeping ([Intapp Time](https://www.intapp.com/time-tracking/),
   [Intapp Time Horizon Cloud](https://www.intapp.com/blog/time-horizon-cloud-legal-timekeeping/)).
2. **Aderant iTimekeep / Apollo / Expert ecosystem** - strongest comparator
   for AI-assisted narratives, rule validation, client requirements, predictive
   client/matter assignment, and Outlook capture ([Aderant iTimekeep](https://www.aderant.com/solutions-itimekeep/),
   [Aderant Apollo](https://www.aderant.com/solutions-apollo/),
   [iTimekeep for Outlook](https://www.aderant.com/news-pr/itimekeep-for-outlook-highlights-july-product-release/)).
3. **Elite 3E** - system-of-record comparator for finance/practice management,
   time and billing, and enterprise integration ecosystems ([Thomson Reuters Elite 3E release](https://www.thomsonreuters.com/en/press-releases/2017/may/elite-3e-brings-convenience-of-amazon-alexa-to-time-and-billing),
   [iManage Elite partner page](https://imanage.com/technology-partners/elite/),
   [LawPay Elite 3E partner page](https://www.lawpay.com/partners/elite-3e/)).
4. **BigHand** - relevant enterprise operations comparator, but broader than
   pure time capture ([BigHand](https://www.bighand.com/en-us/)).
5. **Legaltech Hub timekeeping category** - useful secondary map of the vendor
   category, not primary evidence ([Legaltech Hub timekeeping](https://www.legaltechnologyhub.com/topics/law-firm-operations/timekeeping/)).

## Vendor Notes

### Intapp Time

- Intapp describes Time as time tracking for law firms, accounting firms, and
  professional services ([Intapp Time](https://www.intapp.com/time-tracking/)).
- Intapp describes cloud timekeeping through Time Horizon Cloud and connects
  the offering to lawyer time capture and compliance ([Intapp Time Horizon Cloud](https://www.intapp.com/blog/time-horizon-cloud-legal-timekeeping/)).
- Intapp describes AI-assisted time capture for law firms ([Intapp AI time capture](https://www.intapp.com/blog/ai-time-capture-law-firms-3/)).
- Fit for Beep: strongest benchmark for enterprise-grade passive capture plus
  compliance-aware review.
- Integration posture: public product evidence is strong, but public developer
  API evidence was not found in this sweep; likely partner/procurement-led.

### Aderant iTimekeep / Apollo / Expert Ecosystem

- Aderant positions iTimekeep around AI and rule-based validation, client
  billing requirements, and compliant timekeeping ([Aderant iTimekeep](https://www.aderant.com/solutions-itimekeep/)).
- Aderant describes Apollo as AI-assisted timekeeping with automatic capture,
  predictive client/matter assignment, and AI narratives ([Aderant Apollo](https://www.aderant.com/solutions-apollo/)).
- Aderant publishes an Outlook add-in path for iTimekeep ([iTimekeep for Outlook](https://www.aderant.com/news-pr/itimekeep-for-outlook-highlights-july-product-release/)).
- Aderant claims iTimekeep is used by more than 500 firms ([Aderant iTimekeep video](https://www.aderant.com/video/itimekeep-effortless-timekeeping-with-ai-guided-compliance/)).
- Fit for Beep: best enterprise comparator for candidate generation,
  compliance rules, Outlook signals, and prebill readiness.
- Integration posture: treat as enterprise comparator unless partner access is
  available.

### Elite 3E

- Thomson Reuters has positioned Elite 3E in time and billing contexts,
  including voice-enabled time and billing workflow references ([Thomson Reuters Elite 3E release](https://www.thomsonreuters.com/en/press-releases/2017/may/elite-3e-brings-convenience-of-amazon-alexa-to-time-and-billing)).
- iManage lists Elite as a technology partner, showing DMS integration
  adjacency ([iManage Elite partner page](https://imanage.com/technology-partners/elite/)).
- LawPay publishes an Elite 3E partner page, showing payment integration
  adjacency ([LawPay Elite 3E partner page](https://www.lawpay.com/partners/elite-3e/)).
- Fit for Beep: best example of a system of record Beep should integrate with,
  not recreate.
- Integration posture: enterprise/partner path, not a first solo IP slice.

### BigHand

- BigHand markets legal business-intelligence, pricing, workflow, resource
  management, and operations capabilities ([BigHand](https://www.bighand.com/en-us/)).
- Fit for Beep: useful for enterprise legal-operations vocabulary and
  profitability context.
- Integration posture: not a first time-entry export target based on the public
  materials reviewed.

### Centerbase, SurePoint/Coyote, Litify, Filevine

These products are relevant to broader legal operations or practice management,
but this sweep did not find enough timekeeping-specific, developer-useful
primary evidence to rank them above Intapp, Aderant/iTimekeep, Elite 3E, or the
small-firm API candidates. Revisit only if the user selects a mid-market
segment.

## Enterprise Pattern To Borrow

- Passive capture is acceptable only when paired with compliance rules,
  attorney review, and user-visible correction.
- Enterprise products emphasize client billing rules and prebill quality; Beep
  should model those as validation/evidence constraints before export.
- Outlook/M365 capture is a recurring market signal, but Beep currently lacks a
  local M365 driver in this checkout.
- DMS, finance, e-billing, and payments integration should be treated as system
  boundaries.

## Doctrine Implication

Enterprise research makes the system-of-record boundary clearer. Intapp,
Aderant, and Elite-like platforms already occupy the finance/compliance/billing
center in larger firms. Beep should compete on local-first evidence capture,
agent-assisted narrative/candidate generation, and approval-gated integration,
unless the human explicitly chooses a system-of-record doctrine.
