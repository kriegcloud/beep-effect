Folded-in verdict

The supplemental file does not overturn the overlay doctrine; it makes the docket-of-record backstop explicit.

Recommended operating model

Commercial docket plus local checker.

Use a purpose-built docketing system or attorney-reviewed docket as the authoritative record. Build Beep as a local, evidence-backed cross-check, reminder, and agent-approval layer over official feeds.

  • L1 Official feeds are the programmable foundation: ODP, TSDR, EPO OPS, WIPO tables, and CourtListener.
  • L2 Handroll only deterministic US candidate math; buy or human-review broad foreign and litigation rules.
  • L3 Agents can read, propose, draft, and flag discrepancies; attorney approval gates operative changes.
  • L4 Outlook reminders need durable local state, escalation, and a heartbeat that alerts when checks stop.
