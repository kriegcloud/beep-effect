Patent L1 USPTO ODP

Official patent event source. ODP provides Patent File Wrapper data, documents, office actions, and datasets.

Beep's patent poller should use @beep/uspto and official ODP endpoints. Poll sequentially per key; same-key concurrency is blocked.

Operational constraints matter: ID.me/account requirements, x-api-key, weekly quotas, 429 handling, and no fan-out polling.

Free official API/data source; requires USPTO account/API key and quota-aware implementation.

Rule trap 37 CFR 1.7

Weekend/holiday roll-forward can be subtle for maintenance fees: payment timeliness may roll, while expiration remains tied to the statutory anniversary.

If encoded, this needs explicit tests and attorney-visible explanation. It is not a background date helper.

A small-looking date rule can misstate patent status. Treat edge cases as review-triggering, not silent automation.

No vendor cost; implementation cost is tests, attorney review, and audit trail clarity.

Reliability Heartbeat

The dangerous failure mode is not just wrong math; it is a reminder/checker job silently stopping while deadlines are years away.

Every scheduled poll/check/reminder job needs an independent heartbeat/dead-man's-switch and a tested alert path.

Dead-man's-switch monitoring is a first-class requirement, not polish. No heartbeat means no "never miss" claim.

Low-cost/free tiers available from heartbeat vendors; production use should budget for paid alerting redundancy.

Safe handroll US deterministic rules

Defensible first checker set: maintenance fees, office-action response periods, PCT national-phase, and trademark post-registration windows.

Compute candidate dates and cite the trigger/source. Cross-check ODP/TSDR/ptmnfee2. Send disagreements to review.

Do not handroll foreign annuities, multi-jurisdiction extension chains, cascading court rules, or discretionary dates.

No license required for first slice; main cost is engineering plus attorney review. Add vendor cost when scope crosses the danger line.

Do not own Foreign annuities

Foreign renewals/annuities are the classic danger zone: changing local law, currencies, grace periods, restoration rules, and local-agent requirements.

Beep can ingest evidence and reminders, but the rule engine should be a specialist vendor or attorney/vendor-reviewed data.

License or outsource. Do not broad-handroll.

Specialist annuity/vendor pricing is quote-based; WIPO bulk data subscriptions can be paid, and foreign official fees vary by jurisdiction.
