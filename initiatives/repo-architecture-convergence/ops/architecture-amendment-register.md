# Architecture-Amendment Register

This is the authoritative register for unresolved conflicts between execution
reality and `standards/ARCHITECTURE.md` during repo architecture convergence.

## Entry Rules

- Add an entry when a phase discovers a real standards conflict that cannot be
  resolved locally.
- Record owner, decision phase, current containment, triggering evidence, and
  the proposed amendment path.
- Keep the status current until the conflict is resolved, withdrawn, or landed
  as an approved amendment in the standards.
