# Law KG PRD Spec

## Purpose

This initiative captures the product direction for a lawyer-facing local-first
workspace at the intersection of rich-text authoring, versioning, knowledge
graphs, persistent agent memory, and assistant workflows.

## Product Thesis

- Local-first editor and workspace
- Rich document versioning and AI features
- First-class context graph and knowledge-graph support
- Adjacent workflow surfaces such as email and calendar management
- Background or asynchronous assistant work

## Contract

- This packet is still a seed-stage product-definition initiative.
- The preserved prompt in `history/initial-prompt.md` is source input, not the
  sole authoritative spec.
- Any concrete product or implementation commitment should be promoted from this
  packet into a fuller downstream initiative before engineering begins.
