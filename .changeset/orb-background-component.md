Add `OrbBackground` component — a purely decorative, theme-aware backdrop of soft, blurred, glowing orbs (the ambient "aurora" glow used behind hero sections and empty states). Supports `tone` (green/emerald/teal/sky/violet/amber/rose) and `intensity` (subtle/medium/vivid) props, blends additively on dark surfaces and as a soft tint on light surfaces, and gently drifts forever with a one-shot fade-in intro that is automatically disabled for reduced-motion users. Ships with a comprehensive Storybook story.
